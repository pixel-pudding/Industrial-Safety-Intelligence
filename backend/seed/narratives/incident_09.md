# Incident 09 — Contractor Unauthorized Zone Entry

**Zones:** J -> C · **Date:** 2021-07-22 · **Severity:** Near-miss
**Hazard category:** Unauthorized access
**Contributing factors:** escort requirement not enforced, contractor badge scope mismatch, no real-time cross-check against active permits

## Narrative

A contractor badged in at the Main Gate (Zone J) for scheduled work in Zone
E was later found, via CCTV restricted-area detection, inside the Zone C
tank farm perimeter without an escort or an active permit covering that
zone. The contractor stated they had followed signage toward what they
believed was the correct staging area. No incident occurred, but the
individual was present in a zone with active vapor-detection requirements
and no gas-test awareness or training for that environment.

## Root Cause

Gate check-in recorded the contractor's zone authorization but nothing
cross-checked their live location against their authorized zone list in
real time; detection relied entirely on a CCTV restricted-area alert after
the fact rather than a proactive location-permit match.

## Lessons Learned

Worker and contractor location should be continuously cross-checked against
active permit zone authorization, with an alert generated the moment a
mismatch occurs — not discovered later through camera review.
